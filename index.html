To make the fig swirl: Melt the butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir until dissolved. Cut the figs in half and stir in a saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you get a chunky jam mixture. Add salt, stirring one or two times, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small bowl over medium heat, mix milk with granulated sugar until the sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is barely lukewarm. Whisk in the yolk. Put the mixture in the refrigerator and wait for the fig mixture to cool.

the ice cream machine, pour the liquid into the frozen tub and process according to the manufacturer's instructions, for example, let it spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding the mascarpone and the fig jam mixture and the nuts. Continue the treatment for +/- 10 minutes. Pour the semi-frozen mixture into a Pyrex dish or glass bowl. Freeze it for at least 2 hours before serving.
